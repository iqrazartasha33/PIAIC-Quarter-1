### 3. Operators in Python

#### Arithmetic Operators
- `+` Addition  
- `-` Subtraction  
- `*` Multiplication  
- `/` Division  
- `//` Floor Division  
- `%` Modulus  
- `**` Exponentiation  

#### Comparison Operators
- `==` Equal to  
- `!=` Not equal to  
- `>` Greater than  
- `<` Less than  
- `>=` Greater than or equal to  
- `<=` Less than or equal to  

#### Assignment Operators
- `=` Simple assignment  
- `+=` Add and assign  
- `-=` Subtract and assign  
- `*=` Multiply and assign  
- `/=` Divide and assign  

#### Logical Operators
- `and` (Both conditions True)  
- `or` (At least one True)  
- `not` (Negation)
